About

Welcome to my board game recommendations!

This site is my personal collection of the board games that brought me the most fun, and happy memories growing up. I created it with the goal to share these great games. These games have sparked friendly competition, funny moments, and many hours of entertainment with my family and friends. Each game here is one I’ve played before in my life. Whether it’s the fast pace thinking games, or a focused test of memory, every game has brings something unique to the table.

What you’ll find here

Filter by age and number of players – Games are labeled with age and player count.
Sorted by play style – Find games based on what skill you want to improve: memory, precision, spelling, and more.
Real recommendations – These are the games that I actually enjoyed playing and had a lot of fun with.

So scroll around, check out each category, and find your next favourite game!
